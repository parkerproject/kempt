faq

What are Kempt Points?
Use your Kempt Points like cash to book any beauty service at a member-only prices.
What happens if I don't use all my Kempt Points for the month?
No worries! Any unused Kempt Points rollover to the next month.
What else can I use Kempt Points for?
You can use your Kempt Points to purchase monthly featured products at member-only prices.
What about tips?
We recommend bringing cash to the appointment, but some salons also accept credit cards. Tip accordingly, as you normally would.
